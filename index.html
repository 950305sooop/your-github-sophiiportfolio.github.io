About

My name is Sopihi Huang, I was born in Taiwan and moved to the States in 11th grade. I love drawing, sewing, playing badminton, and Designing.

So exciting to start my design learning journey at University of Washington and be part of community!

What I like about design the most is working with people and brainstorming ideas with others. I enjoying the moment when different ideas emerge and spark new possibilities.

I also really love the energy of the communities within UW RSOs. I always get inspired and motivated by attending different club meetings and listening to different stories from excellent people.
